Silence De Couleur

modeled and animated using Maya, this project that explores the relationship between absence and presence through the interplay of color and abstract forms. Initially devoid of color, the shapes drift in a silent void, symbolizing the emptiness of existence. As the animation progresses, color gradually emerges, representing awakening and emotional expression. In the final sequence, all the forms converge into a central nucleus, symbolizing unity and connection, reflecting on the journey from isolation to wholeness.

Digitization Of Nature

Designed, modeled, and animated in Maya, this project explores the harmonious coexistence of nature and industrialization. In this project, nature begins to grow and thrive within the framework of industrial structures, symbolizing the potential for both elements to coexist and support one another. The merging of organic growth with mechanical innovation conveys the idea that progress and nature need not be opposing forces but can instead integrate, evolving together into a balanced future.
